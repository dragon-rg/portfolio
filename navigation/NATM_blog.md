## What I saw at N@TM
N@tm was a very intriguing experience for me. I got to see the various projects that students in other classes have been working on for this past trimester. I was amazed by all the hard work and dedication that must have gone into each project that my fellow students made.

In the photography class, I saw a variety of high-quality photos that were taken by the students. The 3D animation classes had demo reels that showcased the students's animated projects. And the CSP and CSA classes utilized various computer science concepts to create interactive projects that could be used in the real world.
