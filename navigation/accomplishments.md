### Levels 0-1 for N@TM game

I was the coding lead of my 5-person game-level team for n@tm. We were responsible for our level 0(introduction to the game) and level 1(the first level). I had gotten the idea to create level 0 so that the player was introduced to the story of the game, where the MC had to find the killer of the ghost npc. Both our levels include 8-way movement, interactive dialogue with npcs, and a level-specific background. Level 0 utilizes the sprite sheet for our MC, and level 1 has a new sprite with the MC on a boat.
